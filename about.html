Our Story

LearnHub was founded in 2020 with a simple yet powerful vision: to make quality education accessible to everyone. What started as a small platform with just a handful of courses has grown into a thriving community of over 500,000 students and 1,000+ expert instructors.

Our founders, passionate educators and tech enthusiasts, recognized the transformative power of online learning. They saw an opportunity to bridge the gap between traditional education and the rapidly evolving digital world.

Today, LearnHub offers over 10,000 courses across various domains, from technology and business to creative arts and personal development. We continue to innovate and expand, always keeping our learners at the heart of everything we do.
